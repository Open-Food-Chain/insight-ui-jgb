What is Junglegold?

The first open-source blockchain chocolate bar that encourages socioeconomic and ecological sustainability!

How do we do it? Together with our award winner chocolate producer, Junglegold Bali, we designed a product that is not only empowering local communities in Bali but it is also deliciously eco-friendly! That is because Junglegold Bali locally sources all the components of this chocolate bar and it makes sure that the ingredients come from deforestation-free areas. Meanwhile, the incredibly beautiful design of these bars is inspired by Balinese culture and tradition and made by local artists.

This explorer is currently showing the test Junglegold network!
